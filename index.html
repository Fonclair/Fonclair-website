multidisciplinary sound artist

Dean Strasser or Fonclair approaches his craft with an unwavering desire to grow and refine his artistry, always seeking to deepen his understanding of the relationship between sounds and emotion. Rooted in a rich blend of Luxembourgish and Malagasy influences, his performances are carefully shaped to resonate with diverse audiences. His sets are a thoughtful mix of rhythm, melody, and texture, designed to create an immersive experience that draws listeners in. Having been a staple at De Gudde Wëllen for over a decade, his approach remains humble and ever-evolving. He is known for his technical skills and knack for building tension, as well as his attention to detail and ability to read the crowd, ensuring that each set feels right for the moment. With a genuine love for music and an openness to new ideas, his performances leave a lasting impression without seeking the spotlight, focusing instead on creating a collective journey that everyone can enjoy.

Fonclair expanded his creative horizons as an experimental sound composer and live performer for digital art expositions in Luxembourg and Paris. His work during this period was deeply rooted in exploring the intersection of sound and visual art, crafting immersive auditory experiences that complemented the dynamic nature of digital installations. These live performances pushed the boundaries of conventional sound design, as he used unconventional methods to create textures and atmospheres that brought the art to life. His involvement in these projects allowed him to refine his technical skills and experiment with new forms of expression, further enriching his approach to music and live sets.
